Function: int ap_interval_cmp (ap_interval_t* op1, ap_interval_t* op2)
Non-total comparison.
0 equality
-1 op1 included in op2
+1 op2 included in op1
-2 op1.inf less than op2.inf
+2 op1.inf greater than op2.inf
